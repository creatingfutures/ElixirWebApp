Sentences – Level 1 Lesson

What is a sentence?

A sentence is a group of words that are put together to mean something. A sentence is the basic unit of English. A complete sentence has at least a subject and a main verb to state (declare) a complete thought

  • A new sentence begins with a capital letter
  • A sentence ends with punctuation (a period, a question mark, or an exclamation point)

Sentences – Level 1 Examples

  • My name is Ashwin
  • I like Biriyani.
  • The sky is blue.
  • Today is Monday.
  • Tomorrow is Tuesday.
  • The baby is smiling.
  • He is my friend.
  • I love watching TV
  • Today is my birthday.
