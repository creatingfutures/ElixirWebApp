Sentences – Level 4 Lesson

Type of Sentence

There are four main types of sentences:

  • Simple Sentence
    • These sentences are used to state information. They are the most commonly used sentence type.
  • Command Sentence
    • These sentences give commands or make requests. Imperative sentences end with a period. For example: Open the window
  • Question Sentence
    • These sentences ask questions. Accordingly, they end with a question mark. For example: Did you complete the assignment?
  • Exclamatory Sentence
    • These sentences express emotion and, as such, end with an exclamation mark. For example: She is going to fall!

Sentences – Level 4 Examples

  • Simple Sentence
    • The boy walked home.
    • I love honey.
    • He wants to eat cookies, but he doesn't know how to make them.
  • Command Sentence
    • Please shut the door to keep out the mosquitos.
    • Turn left at the bridge.
    • Stop bothering me!
  • Question Sentence
    • Why does the sun shine?
    • Whose dog is that?
    • Will Shyam get to keep all her lottery winnings?
  • Exclamatory Sentence
    • Wow, he just won a gold medal!
    • What a beautiful house!
    • The birthday cake was so good!
