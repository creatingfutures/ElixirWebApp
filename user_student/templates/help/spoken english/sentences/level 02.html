Sentences – Level 2 Lesson

Simple Sentence

A simple sentence has a subject and ONLY ONE verb:

  • The girl ran after the dog.
  • The cat is sleeping.

Simple sentence = subject + ONE verb

       About Capitals

There are 6 uses of capitals:

1) The 1st word in a sentence or a direct quotation:

For Example:

  • She wanted to go to the beach.
  • She said, "Let's go to the beach."

2) The word 'I' and people's names:

For Example:

  • I feel like sleeping.
  • She said, "Let's go to the beach."

3) The names of specific places, institutions, and languages:

For Example:

  • Did you know that Bishop Cottons is a college in Karnataka?
  • Kannada is the language of Bangaloreans

4) Product names:

For Example:

  • Xerox makes copiers.
  • A smartphone can be an iPhone or a Blackberry or an Android.

5) Calendar items:

For Example:

  • sent my friend a Diwali card while she sent me a Christmas card.
  • Eid falls on the fourth Thursday of June.

6) Titles:

For example:

  • My doctor is named Dr. Sreejish.
  • She always keeps the latest issue of India Today in her waiting room.
