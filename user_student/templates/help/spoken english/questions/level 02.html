Questions Level 2

In this level, you will be learning about the questions which ask for more detailed information. The responses to these questions will give you more details than Level 1. You may come across such questions in your examination or an interview. These are usually referred to as TED questions. T stands for “Tell Me”. E stands for “Explain” and D stands for “Describe”. Questions need not start with TED as seen in the examples below

Examples

  • Describe your summer vacation in a paragraph.
  • Tell me more about yourself.
  • Explain to the audience more details about your project.
  • Give me details on your current roles and responsibilities.
  • Write a letter to a friend describing your examination preparation.
  • What is your idea behind the startup?
