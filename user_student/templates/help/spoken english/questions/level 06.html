Questions Level 6

This level will involve questions which need decision making. We will introduce you to various questioning techniques at this level. These questions collect information and could be used for research purposes. This includes the following

1. Multiple Choice Questions
2. Survey Related Questions

Example

Multiple choice questions

1. Which flavour of ice cream do you like? (select all that is applicable)

  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla
  • Strawberry
  • Pista
  • Orange

2. Which state do you live in? (select only one option)

  • Karnataka
  • Maharashtra
  • Kerala
  • Tamil Nadu

Survey related questions

  • How do you rate the course?
    • 1 being low and 5 being highest (Can add slider/radio buttons)
  • Did you learn something useful today?
    • Yes / No
