Questions – Level 4

In this level, you will learn about questions that will help you analyse based on history or facts. Analysis is an important part of thinking. The responses to these questions need analysis accompanied with facts.

Examples

  • Why is the sky blue?
  • What makes the Earth spin?
  • What is the difference between speed and velocity?
  • What are the planets in our solar system?
  • Why does metal rust?
  • Why did the First World War happen?
