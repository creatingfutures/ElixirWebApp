Writing – Level 1

Writing consists of letters, words, phrases, and sentences. In the beginner level, we would just focus on writing simple one line sentence for communicating an information.

Example

  • If we have to communicate a congratulatory message, then the possible write ups can be,
    • “Congratulations on your promotion!!!”,
    • “You have done a Great job. Congrats!!!”,
  • If we have to communicate a simple message to acknowledge someone or thank someone, then we can have possible write-ups like
    • “Thank You very much!!”,
    • “Thanks!!!”,
    • “Thank you very much for your support and help!!!”
