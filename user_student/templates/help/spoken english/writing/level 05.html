Writing – Level 5

Level 4 would focus on sending emails. In today’s world of technology, gone are the days of hand-written mails. The basic structure of an email involves your name, the recipient’s name, Subject line and Content. Developing basic knowledge on email etiquettes and contents are particularly important these days.

Example:

  • Email to your teacher submitting your project

To: Parimala

From: Shanthi

Subject: Science Project Report

Dear Sir / Madam:

I have attached my project report along with this email. If you have any difficulty opening it or reviewing it, please let me know.

Thank you.

Shanthi M

10 A

  • Email about a business event,

To: Jimmy

From: Abhishek

Subject: Report on Tech Conference

Dear Jimmy,

Thanks for making it possible for me to go to the Tech Conference in Bangalore last week. As you know, it is the biggest technology conference in the country and very important to our work. I learned a lot and I have made some new contacts that will be good for our company. I think we should try to attend the ‘Innovation Day’ in April as well. I focused on talks about robotics, as that is our most interesting area at the moment. The presentations on robot ‘hands’ and robots for hospitals were particularly interesting for us. I am attaching a detailed report on this new research with some suggestions about how we can use it. Thanks again for this opportunity. I am happy to discuss the report further if you are interested.

Best regards,

Abhishek

  • An email about expressing an interest or asking for more information

From: Abhishek

To: grants@bangalorcollege.in

Subject: Scholarship

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my interest in applying for the scholarship that your college is giving for one student.

I have completed my graduate, BBA degree from St. Charles College, securing a percentage of 87. I am eager to do my master’s degree in MBA and I would like to apply for the scholarship to help me continue with my education.

I am hoping that you will consider my application and allow me to fulfill my dream of completing my MBA. I look forward to hearing from you.

Regards,

Abhishek
