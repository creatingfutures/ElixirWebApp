Writing – Level 4

This involves combining a set of sentences together to convey some meaning. In the previous level, we focussed on writing a couple of sentences. In this level, we will focus on writing simple texts, notes, personal introductions etc, which would involve a group of sentences.

Example

  • Question) Give a short introduction of yourself?
  • Answer) While giving an introduction to yourself, a standard set of information, usually required are your name, your address, your hobbies, your job etc. So to frame your introduction, it can be something like:
    • I am Abhishek. I hail from Bangalore. I have been working for Creating Futures since last couple of years. My interests include gardening, music, Singing.
