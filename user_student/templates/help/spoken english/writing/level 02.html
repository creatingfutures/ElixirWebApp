Writing – Level 2

In this level, we would focus on writing a sentence for communicating an information / meaning. A set of words need to be arranged together in a meaningful way to convey the information.

Example

  • “Bangalore is the capital of Karnataka and it is also known as the Garden City”
  • “She presented some topics, related to the company, in the meeting”, “He is very busy in his work, which also involves a lot of travelling”
