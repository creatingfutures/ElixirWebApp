Conversation – Level 4

Giving a Speech

Some use the terms ‘speech’ and ‘presentation’ interchangeably. However, for the purposes of this page, a speech is assumed to consist of speaking only. There is little or no interaction, and no slides or other visual aids.

Preparing for your speech

  • As with a presentation, so with a speech: prior preparation and planning prevents poor performance.
  • One of the best ways to do this is to use stories.

Writing your Speech
