Conversation - Level 1

Conversation consists of the following:
  • Express statements (declarative sentences).
    • End with a period. Example - We offer a 30-day return policy.
  • Ask questions (interrogative sentences).
    • End with a question mark if direct questions. Example - Are the new brochures available yet?
    • End with a period if indirect questions. Example - He asked whether I planned to revise the news release.
  • Give commands and requests (imperative sentences).
    • End with a period.
      • Direct Command. Example - Place your order within two days to receive a discount.
      • Courteous Request. Example - Will you please call me by the end of the week.
  • Express emotions (exclamatory sentences).
    • End with an exclamation point. Example - Always satisfy your customers!
